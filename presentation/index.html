New in v2

💬 Talk to your agents mid-build

Don't wait for a PR to give feedback. Send notes and direct messages to agents while they're working — they adjust in real-time.
Hat tip to Mike De'Shazer for the idea.

📌 Queued Notes

  • 📝 Add context while agent is building: "use the existing auth middleware"
  • 📎 Notes attach to the next dispatch or checkpoint automatically
  • 🔔 Agent receives them when it checks in — no interruption

⚡ Direct Messages

  • 💬 Send live: "skip the email notifications for now"
  • 🔄 Delivered immediately to the agent's active session
  • ✅ Agent incorporates changes into current work

Full chat history preserved per task. Every message, every note, every response.

Under the Hood

Built on proven patterns

Autensa's research and ideation engine is inspired by Andrej Karpathy's AutoResearch architecture — the program-guided autonomous research loop.

📄

Product Program

Like Karpathy's program.md — a living document that instructs research and ideation agents. Defines what to look for, what matters, and what to ignore.

🔄

Iterative Refinement

Research runs inform the next cycle. The program evolves as swipe data accumulates. Agents get sharper with every iteration — not just pattern-matching, but learning your taste.

🤖

Autonomous Execution

Agents don't wait for instructions. They follow the program, execute research passes, generate ideas, and loop — exactly like AutoResearch runs experiments autonomously.

🎛️

Human-in-the-Loop

You steer the program, not every action. Swipe decisions become training signal. The system learns what you approve and calibrates — Karpathy's insight applied to product development.

AutoResearch → autonomous science. Autensa → autonomous product improvement. Same architecture, different domain.
